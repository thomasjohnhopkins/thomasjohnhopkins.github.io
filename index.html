Thomas John Hopkins

New York-based web developer with experience in Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, React.js, and HTML/CSS.

My most recent project is BetterSounds, a web application inspired by SoundCloud. The single page site implements a Rails backend with React.js serving as the responsive front end. Visitors to BetterSounds can upload and stream engaging audio content. Please explore the 'My Projects' tab to view further details concerning this applicaton.

When I'm not seated in front of a computer, I'm out searching for a great cup of coffee or training for my next marathon. Prior to web development, I spent six years working in biomedical research. I had the chance to work with passionate doctors and have my findings published in impactful journals, including Nature Genetics and The Journal of Immunology.

Above all else, I consider myself a storyteller. I take various pieces of information and establish connections between them. Whether it's lines of code or months of experimental data, I'm fascinated by how those items come together in a meaningful manner.
